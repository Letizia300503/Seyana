My city

My audio project is based on the environment I wished I grew up in. My persona is me.Through the audio project I tried to share with you how a regular day looks like in my environment. I considered it as being a healthy environment because everyone is happy and in peace. There’s a mix of cultures and everyone goes along, everyone knows each other and they consider themselves as one big family. What inspired me, is that I wish I could live in a world with no violence and hate. I combined 7 different samples which are going to encounter a regular journey in my neighborhood. The most challenging part of the project was to understand how to use Audacity, but with a little bit of concentration and the teacher's explanation, I was able to figure out what I needed to know for my audio project.
